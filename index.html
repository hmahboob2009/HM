About Me

Hasan Mahboob is a seasoned IT executive with a background in engineering, having worked for prestigious airlines like Qantas, Virgin Australia, and the NSW Government. His strategic leadership has driven innovation and enhanced customer experiences in the aviation industry.

Passionate about environmental sustainability, Hasan is a dedicated advocate for keeping the environment healthy. He actively promotes recycling and has successfully established two community gardens, fostering a sense of environmental consciousness and community spirit.

Hasan's journey from engineer to IT executive showcases his adaptability and strategic vision. His commitment to sustainability and community engagement sets him apart as a leader who not only excels in his professional endeavors but also makes a positive impact on the environment and society.
